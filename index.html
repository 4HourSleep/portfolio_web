ABOUT

About Me

Hello! I'm Keyi Wang, but everyone calls me Finn. I'm passionate about creating and expressing myself through various mediums, combining technology with creativity to bring unique ideas to life.

I'm a student at Goldsmiths University of London, majoring in Digital Arts Computing. I'm also a bass guitarist and a digital artist.

I'm a big fan of punk and emo music, and I'm a big fan of the 70s and 90s. I'm a big fan of the color BLUE&RED.

My Skills

Digital Art, Play bass guitar, Game Development, Web Design...

The aesthetic of my portfolio page, with its bold red and black checkered pattern and distinctive Windows-style interface, reflects my appreciation for creative digital design. My artistic side shows through in my gallery of digital artworks, while my technical skills come through in my programming projects. When I'm not coding or creating visual art, I'm probably jamming on my bass, as music is another vital outlet for my creativity. I believe in bringing together these different forms of expression - art, music, and code - to create unique and meaningful experiences. Feel free to reach out if you'd like to collaborate or just chat about any of these interests!
